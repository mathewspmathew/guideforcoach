## Executive Summary

The objective is to identify a suitable replacement for Matthis Abline. Following a comprehensive review of statistical matches and market intelligence, **Loïs Openda** is recommended as the primary target due to his strong statistical alignment, proven performance at a high level, and favorable injury record. **Benjamin Šeško** is proposed as the backup option, offering significant long-term potential despite a considerably higher valuation.

His proven ability in top leagues makes him the most suitable immediate replacement for Matthis Abline.
